About us

Math Expo 2K25 is an exciting intra-college event organized by the Department of Science & Humanities at KGiSL Institute of Technology, set for April 30th, 2025. The expo showcases the creative and practical side of mathematics through 4 engaging tracks designed to spark curiosity and innovation. From visual displays and working models to interactive demos and challenges, it’s a platform to explore real-world math applications. Math Expo 2K25 is a platform to learn, present, and inspire. Ready to uncover the power of math beyond the classroom?
